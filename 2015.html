Russia Forum New York 2015

Annual U.S. - Russia Conference

U.S. – Russia relations deteriorated to an extremely dangerous level. The state of affairs and how it came to that point underscored the urgent need for proactive diplomacy, mutual understanding and respect of each nation’s geopolitical interests, and initiatives to promote political, commercial, scientific, educational, and cultural cooperation.

The forum's three-day agenda included: March 25, 2015 - Washington, DC: Education and Culture; March 26, 2015 - Washington, DC: Politics; March 27, 2015 - New York: Business, Science and Innovation.

Thank you for joining us in New York and Washington to hear the opinions of leading American and Russian experts, participate in discussions, and present your own ideas and proposals to improve U.S. – Russia relations.
